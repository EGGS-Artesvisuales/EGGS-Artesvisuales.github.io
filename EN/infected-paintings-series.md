<h1 class="titulo">Infected Paintings</h1>
<h2 class="subtitulo">When painting behaves like a contaminated organism.</h2>

<figure class="imagen-con-caption">
  <img src="/assets/img/pes-pint-produccion-Serie01-000b.jpg" alt="Infected Paintings, series by Esteban Garnica" loading="lazy">
  <figcaption>The Saturation / The Plague / Infected Paintings. Painting, waste, altered support and contemporary landscape.</figcaption>
</figure>

<section class="parrafo">
  <p><strong>Infected Paintings</strong> is a series where painting stops being a clean surface and becomes a body affected by waste, advertising, plastic and signs of consumption.</p>
  <p>The work connects skin, territory and image: the pictorial field appears invaded by industrial materials, as if the contemporary landscape had developed visible symptoms.</p>
  <p>This landing summarizes the series for circulation and purchase. The full curatorial page keeps the archive, diagrams and internal structure of the work.</p>
</section>
