The Tangible
Representation of the Material World

       This collection of works reflects the physical and material aspects of my environment. The subject matter is strategically chosen to highlight emblematic elements of contemporary society, selected for their significance and their ability to represent current reality.

The criteria for identifying these emblematic elements include:

  • Constant presence in landscapes.
  • Frequency of use.
  • A novel character, unprecedented in earlier times.
  • Significant environmental impact.
  • Ability to symbolize social, cultural, or technological changes.
  • Redefinition of interactions with the environment.

The series seeks to capture the characteristic traits of contemporary reality, remaining in constant evolution and open to expansion through ongoing research.

This line of work currently includes the following series:

Saturation

Pollution and capitalist production

Technology

Influences on the contemporary environment
