<h1 class="titulo">The Subreptitious</h1>

<h2 class="subtitulo">
  Minimal gestures that intervene in the environment without announcing themselves.
</h2>

<p class="parrafo" style="margin-top: 7%;">
  This category brings together urban practices that act through subtlety. They are interventions that do not seek prominence, but instead provoke a brief interruption in the gaze: small perceptual shifts that reveal tensions, contradictions, or silences in the urban landscape. Through graffiti, anti-advertising, and urban mending, these actions propose a critical dialogue with space and its logics of use.
</p>
