<h1 class="titulo">Muralism</h1>
<h2 class="subtitulo">Light interventions</h2>

<figure class="imagen-con-caption">
  <img src="/assets/img/muralismo/muralismo-intervencion-rana.jpg" alt="Frog mural intervention" loading="eager">
  <figcaption>Small-scale mural intervention adapted to a specific wall.</figcaption>
</figure>

<p class="parrafo" style="margin-top: 6%;">
  <strong>Light interventions</strong> gather smaller-scale mural actions or faster resolutions, where the work adapts quickly to the conditions of each site. These are not minor works in intensity, but more precise operations: specific walls, particular situations, and tighter material economies.
</p>

<p class="parrafo">
  In this modality, the mural can function as a visual accent, a gesture of care, a poetic sign, or the reactivation of an everyday surface. The intervention is measured less by size than by its ability to integrate into the environment and activate an immediate relationship with those who inhabit or cross that place.
</p>

<p class="parrafo">
  Within the archive, this line groups pieces where contextual adaptation, sensitivity to the support, and the relationship with the immediate surroundings form the core of the project.
</p>
