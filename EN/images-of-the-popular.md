<section class="parrafo">
  <p><strong>Images of the Popular</strong> works with characters and codes that move through daily
  life, childhood, media, and collective memory. The popular is understood as a shifting field: a
  shared repertoire capable of awakening affection, recognition, and debate.</p>
  <p><strong>Papelucho</strong> is the first completed work in the projected sculptural line
  <strong>pugliEGGSe</strong>. <strong>Meme Painting</strong> also belongs to this branch, but it
  remains in development and does not yet contain completed works suitable for publication.</p>
</section>
