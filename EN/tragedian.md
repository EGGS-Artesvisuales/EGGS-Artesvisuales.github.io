<section class="parrafo">
  <p><strong>Tragedian</strong> is an instruction-based artwork and re-executable installation. A
  completely inert body —or a sculptural or museum-grade replica— of a tear-gas grenade is attached
  to a neutral white wall with gray duct tape according to the artist's protocol.</p>
  <p>The piece acts as a political and ironic mirror of Maurizio Cattelan's <em>Comedian</em> (2019).
  Food transformed into commodity and spectacle is replaced by an object associated with controlling
  public space. The formal economy of the gesture remains, while its historical and social charge
  changes radically.</p>
</section>

<h3 class="subtitulo2">PROTOCOL AND SAFETY</h3>
<section class="parrafo">
  <p>The work only permits a fully inert body or replica. It may not contain chemical agents,
  pyrotechnic material, propellant, or any initiation, detonation, or activation mechanism.
  Authorized activations depend on the artist's installation and documentation protocol.</p>
  <p>The published images are preliminary visualizations made with generative AI assistance; they
  are not yet records of a definitive physical installation.</p>
</section>
