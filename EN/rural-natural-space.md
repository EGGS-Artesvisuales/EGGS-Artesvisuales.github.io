<p class="parrafo">
  This area introduces <strong>Rural / Natural Space</strong> within <em>Displacement of Painting</em>, focusing on works that move pictorial gesture into open, rural, and natural environments.
</p>

<p class="parrafo">
  Printed paper and advertising material are placed in contrast with green and vegetal landscapes. The contamination becomes visible, but the intervention remains temporary: the material is removed after the process, avoiding the production of real waste in the territory.
</p>
