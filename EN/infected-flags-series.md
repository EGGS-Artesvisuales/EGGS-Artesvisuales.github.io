<p class="parrafo">Infected Flags takes a recognizable symbol and subjects it to pictorial contamination. The flag no longer works as a stable emblem: it becomes an altered surface where territory, identity, illness and painting collide.</p>

<p class="parrafo">The series belongs to Infected Paintings and reads the national image as a vulnerable body. Available prints offer a direct acquisition path through limited editions, signature, numbering, dry stamp and EGGS-Studio certificate.</p>
