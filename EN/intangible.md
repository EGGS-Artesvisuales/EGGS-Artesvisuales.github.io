<h1 class="titulo">The Intangible</h1>
<h2 class="subtitulo">Images, values, and memories that organize experience</h2>

<p class="parrafo" style="margin-top: 6%;">
  Within <strong>World Representation</strong>, the intangible names what cannot be exhausted by a
  material object: systems of value, memories, symbols, identities, and narratives that shape
  collective life. The works in this branch make those forces visible through photography, action,
  installation, muralism, sculpture, and documentation.
</p>
<p class="parrafo">
  The research is organized into three related lines. <strong>Images of Capitalism</strong> examines
  labor, power, consumption, and economic circulation; <strong>Images of the Popular</strong> works
  with characters and codes from shared memory; and <strong>Images of the Ancestors</strong>
  addresses identity, territory, and cultural continuity. Circular nodes highlight the active route.
</p>
