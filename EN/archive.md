<h1 class="titulo">EGGS-Studio Archive</h1>

<h2 class="subtitulo">A space for exploring processes, documentation, and references</h2>

<p class="parrafo" style="margin-top: 6%;">
  Here you will find a collection of documentary material that reflects my creative process at different stages: sketches, records of works, catalogs, writings, and archives that accompany the development of projects.
</p>

<p class="parrafo">
  This archive is constantly being built and expanded. Its purpose is to offer open access to references, research, documents, and visual material that help understand the path of the works and their context.
</p>
