<h1 class="titulo">Images of Capitalism</h1>
<h2 class="subtitulo">Critical representations of the economic system</h2>

<section class="parrafo">
  <p>
    This series brings together works that examine the impact of capitalism on people, the environment, and social structures. Through photography, drawing, and art action, the pieces propose a visual reflection on the dynamics of power, exploitation, and consumption that define the current economic system.
  </p>
  <p>
    Each work is articulated from a specific point of view: <strong>El Colgado</strong> exposes the alienation of the worker; <strong>Mr. Burns</strong> embodies corporate greed; and <strong>El Empaquetador</strong> denounces the environmental saturation produced by everyday consumption. Together, these images open a space of critique and awareness toward the invisible structures that govern us.
  </p>
</section>
