<h1 class="titulo">Exhibitions</h1>

<p class="parrafo" style="margin-top: 6%;">
  This section brings together a selection of exhibitions, festivals, residencies, and presentations in which I have participated over the years. It includes solo and collective exhibitions, as well as training experiences and context-based work that have been key to the development of my practice and to the creation of bonds with different territories and communities.
</p>

<p class="parrafo">
  The list is not intended as a closed archive, but as a record in constant update: a way to show processes, collaborations, and spaces where the work has unfolded and encountered new perspectives.
</p>
