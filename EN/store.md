  <h2 id="editions-circulation">Editions and circulation</h2>
  <p>EGGS-Studio distinguishes artistic research, original works and their forms of circulation. Editions, publications and reproductions broaden access to selected images while helping sustain the continuity of the work.</p>
  <p>This circulation does not erase the tension between art, consumption and commodity. It acknowledges that tension as part of the material conditions through which the work is produced, shared and placed in new contexts.</p>
