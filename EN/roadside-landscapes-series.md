<h1 class="titulo">Roadside Landscapes</h1>
<h2 class="subtitulo">Landscape seen through transit, speed and memory.</h2>

<figure class="imagen-con-caption">

<p class="parrafo">Roadside Landscapes brings together images where territory appears crossed by speed, displacement and memory. This is not a contemplative landscape: it is landscape seen while moving, retaining fragments and rebuilding the visual experience of the road.</p>

<p class="parrafo">The series connects painting, visual record and limited edition. Each available print is an accessible entry into the body of work, produced as a giclée edition, signed, numbered, dry-stamped and certified by EGGS-Studio.</p>
