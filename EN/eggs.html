EGGS-Productions & Art Spa
Discover Everything We Offer

E.GG.S - Productions & Art SpA is an innovative Chilean company (Tax ID: 77.973.201-0) that integrates art, technology, and cultural management to transform spaces. Founded by visual artist Esteban Garnica Sánchez, we are committed to creating impactful artistic experiences.

We believe that art is a catalyst for transformation—capable of revitalizing urban spaces, strengthening territorial identities, and democratizing access to culture through comprehensive solutions.

Our experience and versatility enable us to address various needs in the artistic and cultural realm, offering services ranging from muralism and heritage restoration to scenography design, multimedia production, and artistic software development.

Discover how we can transform your environment and enhance the cultural impact of your projects. We are here to collaborate and turn art into a powerful tool for social change.

Let’s transform spaces together through art!
