<h1 class="titulo">Collaborate</h1>

<h2 class="subtitulo">Ways to support projects, community and public work.</h2>

<p class="parrafo" style="margin-top:6%;">
  EGGS-Studio brings together artworks, community processes, city projects and cultural actions that can grow through support from people, institutions, brands, communities and cultural spaces.
</p>

<p class="parrafo">
  You can collaborate by funding a project, offering a wall or space, contributing materials, inviting a workshop or activity, building a partnership or requesting a proposal for your community, institution or territory.
</p>
