<h1 class="titulo">Teaching</h1>

<h2 class="subtitulo">Workshops, programs, and art education experiences</h2>

<p class="parrafo" style="margin-top: 6%;">
  Since earning my degree from the Universidad de Chile in 2011, I have had the opportunity to teach in different contexts and territories. I began this work at a young age, guided by the same passion for art that drives my creative practice. I understand teaching as a continuous exchange, where the person who teaches also learns from the people, places, and stories that meet in each workshop.
</p>

<p class="parrafo">
  Each teaching experience has been a space of mutual learning that enriches both my artistic practice and my way of understanding the world. This page brings together some of those processes: community workshops, institutional programs, school subjects, and special projects around visual arts and muralism.
</p>
